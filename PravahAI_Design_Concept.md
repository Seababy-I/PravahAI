# PravahAI Frontend Redesign V2 — Product Experience First

The current redesign still feels like a dashboard.

I want to shift the platform away from a traditional admin dashboard and toward a premium product experience combined with an operational command center.

IMPORTANT:

* Frontend only.
* Do NOT modify backend logic.
* Do NOT modify APIs.
* Do NOT modify calculations.
* Do NOT modify SERI.
* Do NOT modify forecasting.
* Do NOT modify shadow events.
* Do NOT modify hotspots.
* Do NOT modify learning engine.
* Do NOT modify Gemini integration.

This is purely a UX/UI restructuring task.
